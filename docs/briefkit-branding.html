01 — Logo

The Mark

A document at the center with smaller elements radiating outward — representing a single brief that expands into a complete kit of project deliverables. Warm, organized, and purposeful.

BriefKit

✓ Do

Use on clean backgrounds. Maintain clear space equal to at least ¼ the logo width on all sides. Always pair the icon with the wordmark for first impressions.

✓ Do

Use the icon alone for favicons, app icons, and tight UI spaces. The amber gradient background is the default — use dark/branded variants only when context requires.

✗ Don't

Don't rotate, skew, or rearrange the elements. The relationship between the main document and its surrounding pieces is central to the brand — keep it intact.

✗ Don't

Don't recolor the gradient or place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds. Don't use below 24px — switch to a simplified favicon at small sizes.
